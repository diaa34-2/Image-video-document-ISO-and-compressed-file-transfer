This tool is designed to efficiently locate, collect, and transfer images and videos from your system.
If you are overwhelmed by scattered files or unable to remember where your photos and videos are stored, this tool provides a practical and reliable solution.
It gathers all images and videos into a single folder on your desktop, making it easy to organize your media, transfer it to another device or a new operating system, or create secure backups before formatting.
The tool is fully reliable, open source, and completely free of viruses or malicious software.
